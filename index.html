TRON Token Standards

TRC-10 and TRC-20 are the most common token standards on TRON. TRC-20 supports smart contracts similar to ERC-20.

Connect Your Wallet

Use WalletConnect or browser extensions like TronLink to connect securely. Always verify connection requests.

Risks

Crypto assets are volatile. Never share private keys and always verify wallet addresses. Use hardware wallets for maximum security.
